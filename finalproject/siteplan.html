TreeTribe Site Plan

WDD 231 | Week 5 Project

1. Site Name

TreeTribe

2. Site Purpose

TreeTribe is a community-based website promoting environmental awareness through tree planting. It aims to educate, connect, and inspire individuals and communities to plant and care for trees to beautify the country and improve air quality.

3. Scenarios

  • Where and when is the next community tree planting event?
  • How do I join TreeTribe and participate in activities?
  • What are the benefits of planting trees in my community?

4. Color Scheme

Use Color Hex Code

Secondary Warm Brown #8B5A2B
Accent Teal #008080

5. Typography

  • Headings: Montserrat Bold
  • Body Text: Nunito
  • Logo/Decor: Optional handwritten style

6. Wireframe Description

Homepage (index.html)

  • Hero section with logo, slogan, and call-to-action button
  • Mission statement overview
  • Navigation bar (Home | About | Events)
  • Footer with contact info and social links

About Page (about.html)

  • Why trees matter
  • Tree types ideal for planting
  • Environmental benefits and stats

Events Page (events.html)

  • List of upcoming tree planting events
  • Info on how to join TreeTribe
  • Gallery or testimonials from past events
